Data Cleaning

Before running the regression, the data has to be screened and cleaned. First of all, missing data has to be addressed. Countries with missing data on the outcome measure, i.e., the total adult literacy rates, will be excluded from subsequent analysis. Such decision was made to avoid creating any bias in drawing conclusions from the results. It could be argued that the data set will be less representative of the world population, but such trade-off is unavoidable and future improvements could be made by collecting more data.

A total of 32 countries were left after applying the exclusion criteria.
